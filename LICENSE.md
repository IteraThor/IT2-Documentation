# IT2 Autodarts Scoring System - Licensing & Community Support

This document outlines the licensing terms and community support guidelines for the IT2 Autodarts Scoring System.

---

## 1. Commercial License

*   **For-Profit Sales:** Selling this design for profit requires an active commercial license, available via my [Makerworld Profile](https://makerworld.com/en/@HipsThor/). 
*   **Validity:** Sales are only permitted while the subscription remains active.

## 2. Non-Commercial Exceptions

*   **Personal & Social:** Sharing the system with friends, family, or your local dart club (at material cost only) is expressly encouraged and does **not** require a license. I only ask for feedback or a small donation if you find the project useful.
*   **Community Assistance:** Printing for community members who do not own a 3D printer (at material cost + a small handling fee) is permitted. However, this **MUST** be handled transparently and discussed with me (**IteraThor**) on Discord first to ensure alignment with project values.
